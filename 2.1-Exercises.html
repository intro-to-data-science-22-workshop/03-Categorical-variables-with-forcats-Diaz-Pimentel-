2) Manually ordering levels

In general, income levels are in the order. But, let’s supposed that we work with a reshuffled income.

reshuffled_income <- gss_cat$rincome %>%
  fct_shuffle()

levels(reshuffled_income)
##  [1] "$7000 to 7999"  "$6000 to 6999"  "No answer"      "$3000 to 3999" 
##  [5] "$4000 to 4999"  "$15000 - 19999" "Don't know"     "$10000 - 14999"
##  [9] "Refused"        "$8000 to 9999"  "Not applicable" "$1000 to 2999" 
## [13] "$20000 - 24999" "$25000 or more" "Lt $1000"       "$5000 to 5999"
